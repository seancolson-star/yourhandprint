ABOUT YOUR HAND PRINT.COM

Sean Colson has had extensive experience in sculpture as well as in creating cast concrete historical statuary reproductions.   He reproduced the elegant 1920's Avondale Urns, which featured a  circle of roaring lions at the base. These Avondale Urns once lined the streets of early Sarasota, and were handsome symbols of the Ringing era.  (photo)  

 

Sean created life sized concrete Lions to guard the door of a private home on Lido Key.(photos)  

 

He reproduced and cast a concrete Cross for a local church. (photo)  

 

In addition, he recreated several Charles Ringling Flower Boxes for the

University of South Florida. (photo)

  

THE Ringling Museum hired Sean to do numerous repairs on their outdoor  concrete Statuary. He was also called upon to recondition the Olive Oil Planters found in the gardens at this splendid museum. (photo)  

 

 YOUR HAND PRINT paver incorporates Sean's years of experience working with high quality cast concrete. The result is a uniquely personal design that is suitable for exterior use, and one that will last.
